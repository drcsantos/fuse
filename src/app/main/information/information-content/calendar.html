The Calandar section is for displaying items in terms of time. The main item it tracks is appointments for residents.


Creating an Appointment

Click on the day of the desired appointment and a window will pop up. Fill out atleast the following fields:

  • Reason
  • Resident
  • Location
  • Date
  • Time
  • Transportation
Then click the save button at the bottom of the window.


Updating an Appointment

Click on the appointment that needs to be updated. The window with all the appointments infomation will pop up. Edit any field and click save.


Canceling an Appointment

Click on the appointment that needs to be canceled. The window with all the appointments infomation will pop up. Click a checkbox at the bottom to cancel an appointment. This will cause the appointment to appear in red on the calendar.
