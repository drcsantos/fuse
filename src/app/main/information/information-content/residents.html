Creating a Resident

Click on the "Add Resident" button in the top left

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Sex
  • Building Status
Then click the save button at the bottom of the window.


Updating a Resident

Select the catagory that needs to be updated in the menu of categories to the left. Then click the update button on a resident that needs to be updated.
