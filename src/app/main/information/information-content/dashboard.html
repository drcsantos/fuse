The Dashboard is a summary and control point of the community.


Dashboard Tab

The summary of the community with initially three displays of todays appointments, number of residents in the building, and number of open issues.
Then a table of statistics based on residents in the community that cover the following:

    Then a table of statistics based on residents in the community that cover the following:
  • Average Age
  • Average Time Stayed

Lastly a map showing where all the residents in the community have moved from


Community Members Tab

Displays the users that are part of the community. Do all the following from this tab:

  • Remove members from the community
  • Change access levels
  • Recover confidential issues


Pending Members Tab

Access users that are requesting to join your community.


Community Settings Tab

Change settings about the community that would include:

  • Billing information
